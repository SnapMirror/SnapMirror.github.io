Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

SnapMirrorHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the AirCast application (the "App"), a screen mirroring solution that enables wireless display and casting from your mobile devices to Smart TVs and other display devices.

1. Overview

AirCast is designed to provide seamless screen mirroring and wireless display capabilities. We prioritize your privacy and are transparent about what information we collect and how we use it to enhance your screen casting experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device and Network Information

To enable screen mirroring functionality, we collect:

  • Device Identifiers: Device model, manufacturer, operating system version, and unique device identifiers necessary for establishing casting connections
  • Network Information: Local network configuration, IP addresses, and network capabilities required to discover and connect to Smart TVs and display devices on your local network
  • Display Information: Screen resolution, orientation, and display capabilities to optimize casting quality

2.2 Usage Data

  • Casting Sessions: Duration, frequency, and technical parameters of screen mirroring sessions (we do NOT collect or view the actual content you cast)
  • App Interactions: Features used, settings preferences, and navigation patterns within the app
  • Performance Metrics: Connection quality, latency, frame rates, and error logs to improve service reliability

2.3 Technical Data

  • Crash Reports: Diagnostic information when the app experiences technical issues
  • Connection Logs: Records of successful and failed connection attempts to assist with troubleshooting

Important: AirCast does NOT record, store, or transmit the actual content you mirror to your TV. We only collect technical metadata necessary to facilitate the screen mirroring connection. Your photos, videos, presentations, and other content remain private.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Enable Core Functionality: Establish and maintain screen mirroring connections between your devices and Smart TVs
  • Optimize Performance: Improve casting quality, reduce latency, and enhance connection stability
  • Device Discovery: Detect compatible Smart TVs and display devices on your local network
  • Troubleshoot Issues: Diagnose and resolve technical problems, connection failures, and compatibility issues
  • Develop New Features: Understand usage patterns to develop new screen mirroring capabilities
  • App Improvements: Analyze performance metrics to enhance user experience and app stability
  • Customer Support: Provide technical assistance when you contact our support team

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party analytics and hosting services that help us operate the app (under strict data protection agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Security Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of SnapMirrorHub, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

5. Local Network Access

AirCast requires access to your local network to discover and connect to Smart TVs and casting devices. This is essential for screen mirroring functionality. Network scanning is performed only on your local network and is never used to access devices outside your private network.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission during screen mirroring sessions
  • Secure local network communications using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Technical logs and performance data are typically retained for 90 days unless needed longer for troubleshooting or legal compliance.

8. Children's Privacy

AirCast is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

9. Third-Party Devices

AirCast connects to third-party devices such as Smart TVs. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these device manufacturers. Please review the privacy policies of your Smart TV and other connected devices.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and optional data collection in app settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@snapmirror.hub

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes through the app or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of AirCast after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
